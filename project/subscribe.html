  Bake For Good


Want to bake for a good cause? Join me in King Arthur Flour's "Bake for Good" to help donate meals through Feeding America. It is the nation's largest organization dedicated to fighting domestic hunger.

So how can you get involved?

Buy a Mix, Give a Meal In a rush? Buy an Essential Goodness mix and King Arthur Flour will make a donation to Feeding America.
Bake for Good Kids Join a free program that teaches kids how to bake bread. One to enjoy and one to share.
Bake with Kids Learn to bake whole-wheat bread with a step-by-step instructional video.
