About


Hi!


I'm Lauren. I'm just an amateur baker from San Diego, California that happens to bake and blog on the weekends.


This blog is new summer hobby that started near the end of middle school. I would sit in my classes, pretending to take notes on Shakespeare, when I was actually browsing through food blogs and coming up with ideas on how to start my own. Before the beginning of the next school year, I finally decided to create this blog to document any new recipes or recipe modifications I developed.


I hope you guys have as much fun trying out these recipes as I did making them!
